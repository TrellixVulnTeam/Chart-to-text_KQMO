The typical (median) American owned two of the above devices and 17% said they only owned one of these devices. Some 77% of single-device owners said their one device was a cell phone. Another 17% of those with one device said it was a desktop, while 6% said it was a laptop. Fewer than 1% of these one-device owners said their only device was an e-reader, and none said that their one device was a tablet computer.
