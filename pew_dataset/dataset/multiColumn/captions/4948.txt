With the holiday shopping season underway, about twice as many are hearing good news about retail sales (28%) as bad news (13%); about half (48%) are hearing mixed news. This sentiment is roughly in line with opinions measured in December of each of the previous two years.
Despite the Dow Jones Industrial Average trading near record highs, attitudes are mixed when it comes to recent news about the stock market.
