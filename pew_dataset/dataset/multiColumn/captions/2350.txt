Overall, the public has mixed views about U.S. efforts to solve world problems: About equal shares say U.S. does too little (33%), too much (30%) and about the right amount (29%) in helping to solve world problems.
Current views mark a shift from April 2016. Two years ago, a 41% plurality said the U.S. did too much to solve global problems; fewer said the country did too little (27%) or about the right amount (28%).
