The survey also found a widespread belief among LGBT adults that the Obama administration, and the Democratic Party, are generally supportive of LGBT people: 63% said the Obama administration was generally friendly toward lesbian, gay, bisexual and transgender people; while just 6% said the administration was generally unfriendly (30% said it was neutral). LGBT views of the administration track closely with views of the Democratic Party (57% friendly). By contrast, 76% of LGBT adults say the Republican Party is unfriendly toward lesbian, gay, bisexual and transgender people.
