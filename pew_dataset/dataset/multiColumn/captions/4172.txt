When it comes to how Maduro is handling various aspects of his job, Venezuelans offer harsh criticism. Maduro’s worst issue ratings are on inflation (86% disapprove) and crime (85%). Reports indicate an increase in the country’s crime rates, which some attribute to the shortages of basic goods plaguing the country. Roughly eight-in-ten also give Maduro poor marks for his handling of corruption (83%) and unemployment (80%). And 77% disapprove of how he is dealing with political opposition.
