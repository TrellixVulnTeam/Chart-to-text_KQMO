The same report found that Americans overwhelmingly support limits on campaign contributions and believe new laws could reduce the role of money in politics. Around three-quarters of U.S. adults (77%) said there should be limits on the amount of money that individuals and groups can spend on political campaigns, while 20% said individuals and groups should be able to spend as much as they want. And around two-thirds of Americans (65%) said new laws could be written to effectively reduce the role of money in politics, while 31% said such laws would not be effective.
